Answers to Your Questions

What is the primary AI function?

Our AI offers two main functions: a chatbot for querying relationships between diseases, chemicals, pathways, and genes, and an advanced alignment solution for comparing protein similarities between species.

How does the chatbot help bioinformaticians?

The chatbot acts as an intelligent assistant, rapidly providing connections and information on complex biological entities, streamlining research and hypothesis generation.

What does the alignment AI do?

The alignment AI compares protein sequences across different species to identify similarities, evolutionary relationships, and functional domains with high precision and speed.

Is the alignment tool for human vs bacteria only?

No, our alignment tool supports comparisons across a wide range of species including mammals, plants, fungi, and microorganisms, enabling comprehensive cross-species analysis.

Can I get data on specific pathways?

Yes, our chatbot can provide detailed information about metabolic pathways, signaling cascades, and gene regulatory networks, including their interactions with diseases and chemicals.
